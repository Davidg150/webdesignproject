Welcome to Templeogue Basketball Club

Templeogue Basketball Club is an Irish basketball club based in Dublin. Templeogue is one of the largest basketball clubs in Ireland, with over 300 members and 20 teams competing in Dublin Leagues and National Competitions. The club's senior men's representative team, Black Amber Templeogue, plays in Ireland's top national league, the Super League.
